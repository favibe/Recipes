Yam and Egg Sauce Recipe

Description

This boiled yam and egg sauce combo is a simple but satisfying Nigerian meal. The egg sauce is made with tomatoes, onions, and spices for a rich, savory flavor.

Ingredient

  • 1 medium tuber of yam
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 small bell pepper
  • Seasoning cube (e.g., Maggi or Knorr)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the yam and cut it into thick round slices or chunks.
  2. Rinse the yam, place them in a pot, pour water, and add a pinch of salt to boil.
  3. Cook until soft (about 15-20 minutes). Then drain and set aside.
  4. Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt, and beat lightly.
  5. Heat oil, saut√© onions, then add tomatoes, bell pepper, and scotch bonnet. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Season with seasoning cube, salt, and pepper.
  7. Pour in the eggs, let them set, then stir and cook until done but moist.
