Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This Nigerian fried rice is a colorful and tasty dish made with mixed vegetables, rice, and seasoned spices. It's often served with grilled chicken or fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced liver or cooked chicke
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and liver/chicken and stir-fry
  2. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Season with curry powder, salt, and a seasoning cube.
  4. Add the cooked rice, mix well, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
